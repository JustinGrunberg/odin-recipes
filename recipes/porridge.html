Porridge.

Description.

Protein porridge is a nutritious breakfast option that combines oats with a source of protein, such as protein powder or Greek yogurt, for a filling and balanced meal. The oats are cooked in water or milk until creamy, then mixed with protein and sweeteners like honey or fruit for added flavor and nutrition.

Ingredients.

  • Oats
  • Almond milk
  • Berries
  • Honey
  • Protein
  • Nuts

Steps.

  1. Cook Oats: Combine oats with water or milk in a pot. Cook over medium heat, stirring until thick and creamy.
  2. Add Protein: Stir in your choice of protein powder, Greek yogurt, or a scoop of nut butter until fully mixed.
  3. Sweeten: Add sweeteners like honey, maple syrup, or mashed banana to taste.
  4. Serve: Top with fresh fruit, nuts, or seeds, and enjoy warm.
