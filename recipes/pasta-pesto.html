Pasta Pesto

Description:

Pasta with Pesto alla Genovese is a traditional Italian dish from Genoa featuring a vibrant green sauce
made from fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil. The ingredients are blended into a smooth,
fragrant pesto that is then tossed with al dente pasta, typically linguine or trofie. The dish is often garnished
with extra Parmesan and sometimes a drizzle of olive oil, delivering a fresh, nutty, and aromatic flavor profile.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Pesto alla Genovese
  • Parmesan

Steps:

  1. Boil Pasta: Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water, then drain.
  2. Warm Pesto: In a large pan, lightly warm the pesto from the jar over low heat (optional).
  3. Mix Pasta: Toss the cooked pasta in the warmed pesto, adding reserved pasta water to achieve desired consistency.
  4. Serve: Plate the pasta and top with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil, if desired.
